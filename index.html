About

TheFactoryMustGrow offers a unique and captivating gaming experience that combines elements of survival with industrial strategy. Players embark on their journey waking up on an uninhabited island, far from civilization. From the very beginning, players feel the pressing need for food and shelter, facing the harsh realities of the wild.

Survival and Development

The game starts with the crucial task of survival. Gathering food from bushes, cultivating crops, and hunting wild animals, players must meet their basic needs for food and water. However, TheFactoryMustGrow goes beyond traditional survival games by presenting a complex challenge - constructing a full-scale production complex on the island.

Factory Construction

The player's goal is not merely to survive but to build an advanced factory capable of producing the necessary technologies for evacuation from the island. This process requires collecting resources, building machines, creating automated production lines(not implemented yet), and developing energy sources.

Advanced Metallurgy System

One of the features of TheFactoryMustGrow is its advanced metallurgy system. Players can explore various methods of mining, processing, and combining metals to create alloys used in machine construction and building materials. This aspect of the game requires strategic planning and a deep understanding of the properties of different metals

Strategy and Creativity

TheFactoryMustGrow poses complex challenges that demand logical thinking, strategic planning, and creative approaches. From constructing basic survival tools to developing intricate production chains and energy systems, players must constantly adapt to changing conditions and find new ways to efficiently use the island's limited resources.
The ultimate goal of the game is to create a mechanism for escaping the island, which requires not only survival and factory building but also a profound understanding of technological processes. TheFactoryMustGrow promises players a challenging yet immensely engaging adventure, where every decision matters, and every problem opens up new opportunities for innovation.
